Gamming Addiction
What is Gaming Addiction?

Gaming addiction is a growing concern that affects individuals across all ages. While gaming can be an enjoyable pastime, excessive gaming can lead to negative impacts on mental health, relationships, and daily life. At FASAA, we recognize the challenges that come with this addiction and are here to help you regain control.

Signs of Gaming Addiction

If you or a loved one is experiencing any of the following, it may be time to seek help:

  • Neglecting responsibilities and relationships
  • Loss of interest in other activities
  • Irritability or anxiety when unable to play
  • Using gaming as an escape from reality
  • Continued gaming despite negative consequences

Pornography

Pornography addiction is a serious issue that can profoundly affect relationships, mental health, and overall well-being. While many view pornography as a harmless form of entertainment, excessive use can lead to compulsive behaviors that disrupt daily life. At FASAA, we understand the challenges of overcoming this addiction and are here to support you on your journey to recovery.

Signs of Pornography Addiction

If you or a loved one is experiencing any of the following signs, it may be time to seek help:

  • Preoccupation with pornographic content
  • Neglecting personal responsibilities and relationships
  • Experiencing feelings of shame or guilt after use
  • Difficulty controlling or reducing consumption
  • Using pornography as a coping mechanism for stress or emotional issues
