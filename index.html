About Us

FASAA Rehabilitation Centre was started in 2023 in response to the Drug and Substance Abuse pandemic that is ravaging our nation Zimbabwe. FASAA Rehabilitation Centre is equipped with cutting-edge technology and staffed by dedicated mental health professionals who are committed to providing the highest quality care to individuals recovering from the adverse effects of abusing drugs and substances. Each client receives mental health care and support from the following mental health professionals:

  • Psychiatrists
  • General Practitioners
  • Psychologists
  • Counsellors
  • Family therapists
  • Psychiatrist Nurses
  • Chaplaincy
  • Life Coaching
  • Social Work
  • Mindset Reprogramming
  • Occupational Therapy

FASAA Rehabilitation Centre aims not only to rehabilitate but also to empower individuals so that they can regain their potential, strength, self esteem and become once again productive members of society.
